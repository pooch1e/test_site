About Me


I am an artist, administrator and facilitator with a passion for archives, research and education. With a wide range of skills and experience, I strive for intersectionality and inclusion in my work, trying to involve social practice where I can. I have experience in large art festivals, gallery environments, film and television production and administration and am excited to explore these opportunities in the UK.
